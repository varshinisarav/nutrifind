Ingredients

boneless chicken pieces, yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, salt, cornflour, rice flour, oil for deep frying, fresh curry leaves, green chilies, garlic, lemon wedges, and fresh coriander for garnish.

Recipe

Chicken 65 is a popular spicy, deep-fried chicken dish from South India. To prepare it, marinate chicken pieces in a mixture of yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, and salt for at least an hour. Coat the marinated chicken with cornflour and rice flour, then deep-fry until golden and crispy. In a separate pan, sauté curry leaves, green chilies, and garlic in oil, then toss the fried chicken in this mixture. Serve hot, garnished with lemon wedges and fresh coriander.

Recipe

To prepare Chilli Chicken, marinate boneless chicken pieces with soy sauce, green chili sauce, red chili sauce, vinegar, cornstarch, egg, salt, and black pepper for about 30 minutes. Deep-fry the marinated chicken until golden and crispy. In a separate pan, heat oil and sauté chopped garlic, ginger, green chilies, diced onions, and bell peppers until slightly tender. Add the fried chicken, along with additional soy sauce, chili sauces, vinegar, a pinch of sugar, and adjust salt and pepper to taste. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the chicken is well-coated with the sauce. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.

fresh prawns, black pepper powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, ginger-garlic paste, curry leaves, chopped onions, green chilies, tomato, mustard seeds, fennel seeds, cumin seeds, oil, and salt. Optionally, you can garnish with fresh coriander leaves and lemon wedges.

Recipe

To prepare Prawn Pepper Fry, start by marinating the prawns with turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt for about 15 minutes. In a pan, heat oil and add mustard seeds, fennel seeds, and cumin seeds until they splutter. Add chopped onions, green chilies, and curry leaves, and sauté until the onions turn golden brown. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften. Add the marinated prawns and cook for a few minutes until they turn pink and are cooked through. Sprinkle black pepper powder and mix well. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the prawns are well-coated with the spices. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot, with lemon wedges on the side.

Recipe

To make Mutton Chukka, start by cooking mutton pieces with salt and turmeric powder until tender. In a separate pan, heat oil and sauté chopped onions, green chilies, and curry leaves until onions turn golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears. Stir in chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften. Add black pepper powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, and garam masala powder, and cook until the masala is fragrant. Then, add the cooked mutton pieces and fry until they are coated well with the masala. Cook for a few more minutes until the mutton is dry and gets a roasted texture. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

Recipe

To make Paneer Chilli, start by cutting paneer into cubes and marinating them with cornflour, all-purpose flour, salt, and black pepper powder. Then, shallow fry the paneer cubes until they turn golden brown and crispy. In a separate pan, heat oil and sauté chopped garlic, ginger, green chilies, and onions until they turn translucent. Add sliced bell peppers and cook until they are slightly tender. Then, add soy sauce, green chili sauce, and tomato ketchup, and mix well. Toss in the fried paneer cubes and stir until they are well coated with the sauce. Cook for a few minutes until everything is heated through. Garnish with chopped spring onions and coriander leaves. Serve hot as a delicious appetizer or side dish.

Recipe

To make Paneer Tikka, start by preparing a marinade with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, chaat masala, lemon juice, salt, and a little oil. Mix well and coat the paneer cubes (and optional diced bell peppers and onions) with this marinade. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes. Thread the marinated paneer and vegetables onto skewers. Grill or bake them in a preheated oven at 200°C (392°F) for about 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally until they are golden and slightly charred. Serve hot with mint chutney and lemon wedges.

Recipe

To prepare Gobi Manchurian, start by blanching cauliflower florets in hot water for a few minutes, then drain and set aside. In a bowl, mix all-purpose flour, cornflour, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, black pepper powder, soy sauce, tomato ketchup, green chili sauce, vinegar, sugar, salt, and water to form a thick batter. Dip each cauliflower floret into the batter and deep fry until golden brown and crispy. In a separate pan, heat oil and sauté chopped garlic, ginger, green chilies, onions, and bell peppers until they are slightly tender. Add soy sauce, tomato ketchup, and a little water to make a sauce. Toss the fried cauliflower florets in the sauce until well coated. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot as a delicious appetizer or side dish.

To make Chicken Soup, start by heating olive oil in a pot and sautéing chopped onions, carrots, celery, and garlic until softened. Add chicken pieces and brown them lightly. Ingredients

mutton bones (such as neck bones or marrow bones), water, onions, carrots, celery, garlic, bay leaves, salt, black pepper, olive oil, and fresh herbs like parsley or thyme for garnish. Optional ingredients include ginger, turmeric, and other spices for additional flavor.

Recipe

To make Mutton Bone Soup, start by rinsing the mutton bones thoroughly. In a large pot, heat olive oil and sauté chopped onions, carrots, celery, and garlic until they soften. Add the mutton bones, bay leaves, salt, and black pepper, and cover with water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer, partially covered, for several hours until the meat is tender and falling off the bones. Skim off any foam that rises to the surface. Once done, remove the bones and vegetables from the soup. Season with more salt and pepper if needed. Garnish with fresh herbs before serving. Enjoy your nourishing mutton bone soup!

Recipe

To make Creamy Mushroom Soup, melt butter in a pot and sauté chopped onions and garlic until translucent. Add sliced mushrooms and cook until golden brown.
